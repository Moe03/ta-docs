## üí∞ Base Pricing  

Orova Agents charges a **base fee of 10 credits per minute** in addition to the provider's costs.

- For the **Pro Plan**, this equals approximately **4 cents per minute**.  
- The higher your plan, the more credits you receive, which reduces the per-minute cost.

---

<Warning>
We charge a small markup unless you‚Äôre on the **highest plan available**. Only users on the highest plan are charged the **exact provider's costs**. The difference is generally negligible unless scaling to hundreds or thousands of calls.
</Warning>
